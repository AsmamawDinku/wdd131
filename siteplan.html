Website Planning Document

Site Name

Explore McLean Trails

This name was chosen to highlight the site's focus—helping users explore local walking, hiking, and biking trails in McLean, Virginia. It is clear, location-specific, and invites curiosity.

Site Purpose

The site provides information about local nature trails in McLean, VA, including trail difficulty, distance, scenic views, user reviews, and amenities such as parking and picnic areas. It will also offer seasonal tips and event announcements related to outdoor activities.

Scenarios

  • "Where can I find easy hiking trails to visit with my kids in McLean?"
  • "Which McLean trails are best for biking and have rest areas along the way?"

Color Schema

Primary Color: #2F4F4F (Dark Slate Gray – used for headings to reflect nature and depth)

Secondary Color: #E0F7FA (Light Cyan – used for backgrounds to keep the feel fresh and clean)

Typography

Primary Font: "Roboto" (Used for body text – modern and easy to read)

Secondary Font: "Merriweather" (Used for headings – clean and professional)

Wireframes

Mobile View

  • Hamburger menu at top
  • Scrollable list of trails with images
  • Filter by type (hiking, biking, family-friendly)
  • Call-to-action button for event signups

Desktop View

  • Top navigation bar
  • Hero image with welcome message
  • Three-column layout for featured trails, filters, and announcements
  • Footer with contact info and links
